A bit about us ...

It all started in 1904 when two young Australian boys (John and Jack) built a sandcastle. Following their debut success, the pair went on to found John and Jack’s Hostel in Kentucky, USA. After twenty years in the business they broadened their venture, buying up most of the large infrastructure in the state. It was at this point that the idea of Tropical Paradise was born.

John and Jack, one of the wealthiest duos in the history of the planet, wanted a place to wind down after a long week. They both had their weird eccentricities (Jack was weirder), so what better spot could they settle on than a hut in the middle of the Sahara Desert! The year 1976 saw the creation of the first ever Tropical Paradise resort, what we now as Saharan Bungalow. This was a resort where eccentric billionaires could pay an exorbitant price for a weekend away from the toils and tears of working life.

Other resorts followed in the next years, with a Tibetan Cave opening for business in 1981 and the company buying up part of Antarctica so you could pitch a tent there in ’85. These days there are a range of 7 different resort locations to choose from for your next vacation.


We always make sure to donate to charities, with $17,000 USD reported as donations in the five-year period since July 2009. We are the primary donor for the Sassy Cats Research Foundation, an institute dedicated to the research and protection of sassy felines. We have also donated to larger charities in the past, including the Heart Liver Disease Foundation and the Institute for Protection of Addresses.
