Dubai Apartment

ACCOMMODATION

Dubai is the richest, tallest, brightest, sharpest city in the world. There are expensive cars, expensive hotels, and expensive girls.

But one of the primary attractions is our own, the Burj El Arab Hotel, pictured right.

Reaching miles into the sky, it's the perfect place to stay for your perfect honeymoon.

ATTRACTIONS

Dubai is home to some of the most legendary tourist traps to ever exist.

You can climb the Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world at 1/2 a mile high - but be warned, there are only stairs so you might just have a heart attack.

There's even a slide up the top where you can slide for like 20 metres down a slight incline!

Dubai comes alive at night with the vibrant colours of the lasers reflecting through the delightful Dubai Water Fountain.

At times shooting ten metres in to the air, this here is the biggest and most beautiful water feature in the world.

So what are you waiting for? Buckle up and get over here for the time of your life

And don't forget the aquarium!

This aquine beauty is the most exquisite in the world, hosting tonnes of fish and even an eel.

If you don't feel like staring at fish all day long you can visit the restaurant next door where you can eat fish half the time and stare at the fish the other half.
