Saharan Bungalow

ACCOMMODATION

Oh the Sahara Desert - the very name brings back fond memories of when this antique spot was once a vast oasis.

Now it's a bleak desert with a few nomads ranging it, but that doesn't matter, it's history all the same!

If you happen to find a hut in the middle of nowhere, it's ours and you're welcome to stay the night. No-one else will want it.

FACILITIES

Every few miles along the high silk road you'll come across a WC (water closet).

But don't be afraid, they're friendly and won't bite!

But maybe the line of creatures waiting outside it will when you try to push past them. But pay no heed - continue in your endeavours!

ATTRACTIONS

The Richat Structure is one of the Sahara's key tourist attractions.

It was formed over 100 million years ago when there was an alien invasion and a spaceship crashed.

There's still some quicksand areas scattered around from the craft's throttle engines, so do be careful when meandering about.

Gebel Dist is a very old mountain, and not to be confused with the pyramids of Giza. It's likely even older than those!

It's said there's an oasis on top where you can eat fresh dates, however no man has survived it's rocky outcrop to return and tell the tale.

Are you game enough to find out?
