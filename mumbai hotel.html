Mumbai Hotel

ACCOMMODATION

This hotel in Mumbai offers the best accommodation you could ever wish for. The rooms are spacious and there are always complimentary drinks and snacks.

The beds are oh so soft and the pillows are fluffy. There are even chairs! Sit back and relax as you watch TV or gaze out the window at the amazing views Mumbai has to offer.

FACILITIES

The hotel room also comes with awesome facilities that you can enjoy to your heart's content.

There's a bathroom with a flushable toilet and a small shower where you can stand under the unequivocally harsh flow of those flat shower heads we know so very well.

There is also room service - our friendly staff will tend to your every need!

ATTRACTIONS

And don't forget Mumbai's tourist attractions!

There's the Gateway of India (see right) which has stood for over a hundred years. It was built by King George when he came to visit, and you can see it when you come to visit too!

Don't forget to take a selfie to share with the folks back home.

The Mahalakshmi Temple is just over the road too!

One of Mumbai's oldest and most famous temples, it adorns the city which so many people call home.

So come on down and call Mumbai home for a day, your adventurous spirit will be rewarded with kind hospitality!
