ACCOMMODATION

Come along to the legendary Amazon Rainforest in darkest Peru! There's so much to see, including fauna and flora. You can choose to stay in one of our hospitable sequoia treehouses.

The trees are tall and a bit hard to climb, but that's nothing compared to the trouble you'll have finding us in the first place. There's no maps and we've only got one hostel in the whole of South America!

FAUNA

The variety of fauna in the South Americas is truly a sight to behold!

Look around as you clib to the treehouse and you might catch a glimpse of one of these reclusive yet colourful Red-lored parrots.

Do be careful though, as they do bite and can dispose of your eyes in a matter of seconds.

The giant ground sloths are another promiscuous item in the country's diverse fauna list.

If you're lucky you might get to pet one, but be carefull because if you hold them for two long they can suddenly turn violent.

Please resist the temptation to saw off their claws and keep them as souvenirs.
